Frequent Questions

WHAT IS UNIQUE ABOUT YOUR DOJO?
At Azadani Dojo, while we train Budo Karate, we teach the Values of Courtesy, Respect, and Humility.
WHAT AGE GROUPS DO YOU ACCEPT IN YOUR KARATE DOJO?

DO YOU OFFER TRIAL CLASSES BEFORE ENROLLING?
Yes, we offer one-week free trial classes for visitors to experience our training environment and meet our instructors. It’s a great way to see if our Karate Dojo aligns with your goals and interests.
WHAT SHOULD I WEAR FOR MY FIRST CLASS?
For your first class, comfortable workout attire (t-shirt and sweatpants) is suitable. If the student decides to sign up as a member, our dojo provides the Karate Uniform to the student as covered by the sign-up fee.
WHAT IS UNIQUE ABOUT YOUR DOJO?
At Azadani Dojo, while we train Budo Karate, we teach the Values of Courtesy, Respect, and Humility.
HOW OFTEN ARE THE CLASSES HELD, AND WHAT IS THE DURATION OF EACH CLASS?
We strongly suggest our students attend at least 2 sessions a week. We offer multiple classes throughout the week, and the duration of each session varies based on the age group and skill level. Typically, the Karate training sessions range from 45 minutes for kids to 60 to 90 minutes for teens and adults.
